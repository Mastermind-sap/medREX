Problem Statement

It is important for a health care provider to know the past medical history of a patient before administering any treatment. However, it is sometimes difficult for doctors to get this information because the patient is unresponsive or even negligent in disclosing past treatments.

Our Solution

medREX is an interconnected patient data management system that allows hospitals to keep track of the treatments, test results and past medical history of patients, even if they change hospitals.
